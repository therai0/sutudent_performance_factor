About This Project

This project explores the various elements that can influence a student's academic performance. By analyzing attributes such as study habits, attendance, parental involvement, access to resources, motivation level, and more, we aim to understand how these factors correlate with exam scores.

The project leverages predictive modeling to estimate a student's potential exam performance based on these factors — helping educators, parents, and students make informed decisions to improve learning outcomes. Every data point matters; every pattern reveals an opportunity.

What we analyze
  • Study time & attendance
  • Family & parental factors
  • Motivation & peer influence
  • Resources & school type
  • Sleep & physical activity
  • Tutoring & previous scores
